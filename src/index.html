Quotes

Ever wanted to get random quotes from The Bang Theory? Well you are in luck!

By invoking the API at /quotes You can get a list of all the available quotes in the store! Alternatively, calling /quote gives you just a single random quote!

Finally, want to add some custom quotes to the list from your favorite show? Well you most certainly can! Calling (as a POST request) /quotes You can upload your own quote juse pass text: "quote" and speaker: "Sheldon Cooper" You can now find your quote on the list!
